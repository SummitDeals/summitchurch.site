Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications developed and published by Summit Apps ("we," "us," or "our"), including but not limited to Sportsball and any other apps available under the SummitDeals developer account.

Information we do not collect

Our apps do not require you to create an account or log in. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

Information collected automatically

Our apps may collect limited non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • General usage data (e.g., which features are used, app crash reports)
  • Approximate location (country or region level only, used for ad relevance)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)

This information is used solely to improve app performance, diagnose issues, and deliver relevant advertising. It is not linked to your identity.

Third-party advertising

Our free apps are supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their own privacy policy.

You can review Google's privacy policy here: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

In-app purchases

Some of our apps offer optional in-app purchases (for example, removing advertisements). All purchases are processed securely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment information. Transactions are governed by the respective platform's terms of service.

Data from third-party services

Our apps may display data retrieved from publicly available third-party APIs (such as sports scores and schedules). We do not store this data on our own servers and we do not associate it with any individual user.

Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Data retention

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete. Any analytics or crash data collected through third-party SDKs is subject to those providers' own retention policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
